Why we chose these companies

Each chosen company represents a significant sector, allowing us to present a comprehensive analysis of various market segments.

Apple (AAPL) was chosen to represent the technology sector, as it is a global leader in innovative consumer electronics, software, and digital services. Its immense influence on the technology landscape makes it an ideal candidate for illustrating the growth and impact of the tech industry.

Coca-Cola (KO) was selected to represent the consumer goods sector, as it is one of the most iconic and recognizable beverage brands worldwide. Analyzing Coca-Cola's market performance can offer valuable insights into consumer trends and the demand for consumer goods.

Exxon Mobil (XOM) represents the energy sector, one of the foundational pillars of the global economy. As one of the largest multinational oil and gas corporations, Exxon Mobil's data can provide a glimpse into the dynamics of the energy market and its impact on the economy.

Simon Property (SPG) represents the real estate sector, particularly in the context of commercial properties and shopping malls. Understanding Simon Property's data can shed light on the performance of the real estate market and its correlation with economic indicators.

Johnson and Johnson (JNJ)was chosen to represent the healthcare sector. Being a prominent pharmaceutical and consumer goods company, Johnson and Johnson's data can offer valuable insights into the dynamics of the healthcare industry and its responses to various market conditions.

Toyota (TM) was selected to represent the automobile sector, as it is one of the largest and most renowned automobile manufacturers globally. Analyzing Toyota's market performance can provide valuable insights into the automotive industry's trends and innovations.
